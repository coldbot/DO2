Decked Out 2 - Player Rating

Decked Out 2 is a competitive, deck-building, dungeon crawler game created in Minecraft by TangoTek as part of his main base. Here, the players are given points for wins, or subtracted points if the fail in the Decked Out dungeon. The amount of points added or subtracted is modified by how difficult a players run was. The rating system is based formula 2 by Arpad Elo found in his publication.

Decked Out 2 Rating Formula:
R2 = R1 + (K(S-E))

R2 is the new rating after a run. R1 is the players rating before the run. S is 1 if the player succeed or 0 if they failed. E represents the probability of winning against a player. But as DO2 is a single player game, E is calculated per game phase as the overall player success for the difficulty + what level the artifact is at (Table 1). E will modify how much a players rank increases or decreases. The maximum value a player can have added or subtracted to their ranking is K, and it's set to 32. If E equals 0.5, a player will gain or loose 12.5 points if they succeed or fail. If E is high, say 0.8, a player will gain 6.4 points if they succeed or loose 25.6 points if they fail. If E is low, say 0.2, a player will gain 25.6 points if they succeed or loose 6.4 points if they fail. All players start with a rating of 1000.

As E values will likely change a lot during a phase, expect player ratings to also change a lot. A players rating is only fixed for phases that are completed.
