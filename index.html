Decked Out 2 - Player Ranking

Data source: DO2 Tracking (The Dungeon Archive).

The rating system is based on what is used for Age of Empires II, see this Reddit post.

ELO Rating Equation:
R2 = R1 + (K(S-E))

R2 is the new rating after a run. R1 is the players rating before the run. S is 1 if the player succeed or 0 if they failed. E represents the probability of winning against a player. But as DO2 is a single player game, E is calculated as the overall player success for the difficulty + what level the artifact is at (Table 1). E will modify the how much a players ELO rank increases or decreases. The maximum value a player can have added or subtracted to their ranking is K, and it's set to 32. If E equals 0.5, a player will gain or loose 12.5 points if they succeed or fail. If E is high, say 0.8, a player will gain 6.4 points if they succeed or loose 25.6 points if they fail. If E is low, say 0.2, a player will gain 25.6 points if they succeed or loose 6.4 points if they fail. All players start with a rating of 1000.
